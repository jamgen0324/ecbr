Positive or Negative experience when seeking assistance
1. Security cautious. By being cautious of links we can prevent such data breaches from occuring. Even by clicking links that are not safe we are highly at risk of infecting our computers and can spread to connected computers in the network.
2. Click before you click. Be mindful of malicious websites when navigating the world wide web. Some sites will even use dialog windows that is a phishing and can override control from your computer.
3. Validate trust. Be careful downloading and installing stuffs from the internet. This can cause a serious infection than can also infect the whole infrastructure.



Support aspects you have to know
4. What got breached. Why does the type of information exposed matter? Some hackers will offer a ransom for the data that got breached and by validating what type of data got stolen, the business can decide on what action to do. Such personal data can also be a lawsuit case from clients.
5. What should we do? A protocol should be applied in any business on what to do during a data breach. Employees must strictly follow this regulation when such events occur.
6. Check breach support. Check if your infrastructure has any breach support. Usually some businesses hire a security expert and if they do have one it is better to consult them for post-breach activities.

BUSINESS SCENARIO

D&K Books Pty Ltd is a bookstore owned by Mr. Dean Kerr. The business occupies two levels of an office building connected by escalators and lifts. D&K Books employs approximately six sales staff, one operation manager, two administrative officers, a bookkeeper and a marketing manager. They have an Ethernet network consisting of ten PCs (Intel I3 Desktop cloned), two switches, a router and three printers. They use the QuickBooks software to manage their entire business, including sales, inventory, ordering, accounts receivable, accounts payable, payroll and employee management. They also have two EFTPOS terminals one on each floor.
